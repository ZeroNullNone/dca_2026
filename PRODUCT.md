# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A crypto-native investor/operator using a private local dashboard to execute a BTC accumulation plan with live market context, bucket discipline, and recorded spot buys.

## Product Purpose

Turn the BTC DCA plan into an operator dashboard: refresh market signals, read the buy score, see the active bucket, place ladder/recovery/capitulation decisions, and log fills without changing the plan every day.

## Brand Personality

Operator-grade, compact, and direct. It should feel like a working trading ledger, not a glossy crypto site.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-dashboard polish, exchange-app noise, decorative crypto terminal effects, and visual choices that make the score, active bucket, or execution log harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Decision first: score, active action, and bucket state outrank decoration.
- Keep the plan visible so execution stays disciplined.
- Make source freshness obvious before any signal is trusted.
- Use compact controls and predictable tables for repeated weekly checks.
- Preserve the private-tool feel from `crypto_portfolio`: dense, restrained, and operator-led.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Do not rely on color alone for pass/fail or buy/sell state. Keep controls keyboard accessible and motion minimal.
